· I · Flagship

guru

cross-tradition esoteric text engine

Guru ingests primary texts from twenty-one traditions, builds a concept graph of their thematic overlaps, and answers with traceable, per-tradition citations. Vector search finds what sounds alike; graph traversal finds what means alike — even across texts that share zero words.

  • Corpus 21 traditions · ~4,400 chunks · 158 concepts
  • Edges ✓ Verified · ◇ Proposed · ~ Inferred — the model hedges to the tier
  • Store SQLite graph + embeddings. No separate vector DB
  • Reading room guru-web — a Next.js corpus browser over the graph

Tiny minds, single files

tiny-games is a run of experiments in plugging a small AI into one self-contained page of HTML: an adversary distilled from search that reads your defense and strikes its gaps, a scorer that grades your vocabulary as spellcraft, a pocket oracle that reads your tarot spread. Entirely client-side. Nothing phoned home at play time.
